Baked Honey Garlic Salmon

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets(about 6 oz each)
  • 3 tablesppos honey
  • 3 clovesgarlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoossoy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional garnish: chopped parsley or green onion

Instrctions

  1. preheat the oven to 400°;F(200°C)
  2. In a small bowl, mix together honey, mincedgarlic, soy sauce, olive oil, and lemone juice
  3. Place the salmon fillets im a baking dish lined with parchment paper or foil.
  4. Season the with salt and peppe.
  5. pour the honey garlic sauce evenly over the salmon fillets.
  6. Bake uncovered for 12-15 minutes or until salmon flakes easily with a fork.
  7. optional: Broil for 1-2 minutes at the end to carameliza the sauce slightly ( watch carefully to prevent burning)
  8. Garnish with chopped parsley or grren onions and serve hot.
